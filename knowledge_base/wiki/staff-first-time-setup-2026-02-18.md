Staff First-Time Setup
How School Staff Access the Unique Schools System
Written by Sam Support | Last published at: February 18, 2026
Before you can access the Unique Schools system, you must be added to the school’s database and
complete the registration process through the Main School App.
This guide explains:
How administrators add staff to the system
How staff download and register
How to access all platforms
 
Need Additional Access or Permissions?
By default, staff access may be limited depending on your school’s settings.
If you require additional permissions — such as:
Posting News or Notifications without approval
Access to Payments
Access to Attendance features
Or permissions for any other area of the system
A member of Senior Management must email staffsupport@uniqueschools.ie to request
additional access on your behalf.
 
 
Initial Step: Ensure You Are Added to the System
(Admin)
Before registering, a school administrator must add you to the system.
Administrator Instructions:
Go to uniqueschoolapp.ie
Navigate to Administration
Select Add/Edit Students & Staff

Click Add
Select Staff
Ensure all details are entered correctly
If your school app displays the “Login with Microsoft” option, enter email address. 
Click Submit
Once the Admin has you added, follow the registration & access steps below.
 
 
How Staff Register
Unique Schools provides access across three platforms:
Main School App
Administrators App
Desktop Dashboard
Important: 
Registration must be completed through the Main School App first.
 
 
Note
If your school app displays the “Login with Microsoft” option, please scroll to the bottom of this
guide and follow the “Login with Microsoft” registration steps instead of the standard registration
process.

Click to Enlarge Image
 
 
Step 1: Download the Main School App
Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
Search for your school’s name
Download and install the app
Step 2: Register Your Account
Open the app
Tap Register
Select your relation to the school:
Teacher/Staff
Teacher/Staff & Parent/Guardian (if applicable)
Enter:
The mobile number that the admin added initially
Your school email address
Create a password
Tap Submit
You will receive a verification code via SMS and email.
Enter the code to complete registration.
Step 3: Log In Across All Platforms
Once registered, you can log in to:
Main School App
For basic access and school updates.
Administrators App
For approvals, communication, and on-the-go management.

Download links:
App Store (iOS)
Google Play Store (Android)
Desktop Dashboard
For full access to checking Attendance Notes, Sending Communications, Creating Forms,
Payments and so much more.
www.uniqueschoolapp.ie
Note: this is also accessible from a mobile device browser.
